ResumeTailor

Paste a job link → get a recruiter-graded, ATS-safe, one-page resume and a tailored cover letter. Nothing invented — it asks you when it needs facts.

0 Settings — how it runs

Your key is stored only in this browser's localStorage and sent only to api.anthropic.com. Don't use this on a shared computer.

💡 Open one claude.ai chat and keep using the same chat for the whole run — each step builds on the previous one.

1 Your resume & CV

Upload your resume as a PDF or Word (.docx) — the final resume will match your original's formatting and fonts. Or just paste the text. Optionally add your longer CV / master experience list — the tailor picks the most valuable experiences from both for each job.

Pasted/extracted text is saved in your browser. Uploaded PDFs are held in memory only — re-attach after a page reload.

2 The job

Fetch pulls the page text automatically. If a site blocks it (LinkedIn often does), just paste the job description below.

3 Tailoring pipeline

  1. Senior-recruiter analysis — match score, missing keywords, red flags
  2. Rewrite experience — Google XYZ formula, no invented facts
  3. ATS filter + hiring-manager scan — fix what gets skipped
  4. Tailored cover letter
